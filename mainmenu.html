Play Game Instructions
  • Both you and the opponent are
    given 6 cards with 4 of each, 4
    colored cards, and 2 black and white
  • After rolling the dice, you are to
    either pick a colored card, or a black
    or a white.
  • Picking a white when the dice shows
    a colored card, means you would lose 2 white
    cards.
  • Picking "Color" when white is picked
    by the dice, would mean you are picking the
    the white card.
  • To win, you need the white cards. Losing
    all your black or white cards would automatically
    make you lose.
